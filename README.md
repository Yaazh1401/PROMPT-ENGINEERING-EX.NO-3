3. Preceding Question Prompting
Definition

Preceding question prompting uses an introductory or guiding question before the main task to improve reasoning and contextual understanding.

Example 1
Prompt

Why is climate change a global concern? Explain how greenhouse gases contribute to global warming.

AI Response

Climate change is a global concern because it affects weather patterns, sea levels, agriculture, and human health worldwide. Greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide trap heat in the Earth’s atmosphere, leading to global warming.

Evaluation
Criterion	Rating
Accuracy	5
Clarity	5
Structure	4
Response Quality	5
Example 2
Prompt

How do vaccines work? Describe the process of immunization in simple terms.

AI Response

Vaccines help the immune system recognize and fight harmful microorganisms. They introduce weakened or inactive parts of a virus or bacteria into the body, allowing the immune system to build protection without causing serious illness.
